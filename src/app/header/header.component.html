Bound by Blood Program

As an NGO, AWO’s primary operation is donating blood to those who don’t have an easy access to it. By eliminating financial, bureaucratical or circumstantial hurdles, we aim to provide an easier channel for people to have access to blood, in order to save the lives of their loved ones. OUR MISSION AT ARMS WIDE OPEN

At Arms Wide Open, you can find us being proactive with all our core
missions at any given point in time. Since we’re familiar with the cost of losing a life, be it in families or in a society,
all the members of our community are engaged 24x7 in either one or all of these mission mandates:

Donations

With the world becoming increasingly conservative & high-walled, it’s easy for us to close our eyes to others’ misfortunes. Some blame misinformation for this problem while others are not convinced that their contributions are reaching the right people. To act as a solution to these dilemmas, we’ve chosen to act as an honest platform where people can donate resources that are needed by the needy. From blood to non-perishable goods in the face of disasters, we collect items of grave importance so that the people who suffer in times of crisis, are never left behind.

Donate Now

Volunteering

A mission as ambitious & noble as that of saving other people’s lives demands a workforce that is committed to making a difference. At AWO, there will always be times when we’ll need all the help we can get in order to subvert the effects of any crisis. If you wish to impact others’ lives for the good, we promise you that AWO can be a great platform for putting your gifts into action! Like we urge each & every new member that joins us, it’s never too late to save lives.

Volunteer Now

Fundraising

Being an NGO that strives to contribute support towards active social concerns in the country, we monitor various emergencies at any given moment to lend a helping arm. As a result, we host many fundraising operations which can positively impact the lives of all those who’re caught in the crossfires of a crisis. In these circumstances, we like to remind people that your help today goes a long way towards preserving the lives of many others. We invite you to look into our ongoing fundraising campaigns & pitch in where it matters most.

PAN INDIA INITIATIVES

BOUND BY BLOOD

BE A PART OF OUR BLOOD DONATION PROGRAM

Desperate situations arise everyday where people need access to blood, in order to save their loved ones. In order to facilitate this service for them without too many hassles, AWO has risen to the occasion by making blood donation its primary initiative.

SANTHRUPTHI

ELIMINATE POVERTY WITH OUR FOOD CONTRIBUTION PROGRAM

AWO has undertaken ‘Santhrupthi’ - an initiative that will provide excess food (which is fresh but not consumed) from restaurants & other catering services to underprivileged people to combat starvation & malnutrition.

Read More

FAIRYTAILS

SAVE ANIMALS’ LIVES WITH OUR ANIMAL RESCUE PROGRAM

For animals that are discarded by irresponsible owners & societies alike, we at AWO save these creatures & provide them with a loving home under our ‘Fairytales’ animal rescue initiative.
